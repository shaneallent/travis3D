About Travis3D

Who We Are

Travis3D, Inc is a professional 3D design studio based in Charlotte, North Carolina. We specialize in high-quality digital and physical product creation, serving businesses, product designers, artists, and creators worldwide.

Founded on expertise in 3D modeling, rendering, and manufacturing, we transform concepts into stunning visual assets and production-ready prototypes. Whether you need photorealistic product visualizations, precision scanning, custom environments, or functional 3D prints, we deliver professional results that exceed expectations.

What We Do

Our core services include:

  • Product Visualization – High-fidelity renders for marketing, presentations, and advertising campaigns
  • 3D Scanning & Photogrammetry – Convert physical objects into production-ready digital models
  • Custom Environments – Stylized and photorealistic scenes for product showcases and visual effects
  • 3D Printing & Prototyping – From design optimization through finished products with professional post-processing

Why Choose Us

  • Professional expertise in 3D design, rendering, and manufacturing
  • Custom solutions tailored to your specific needs and budget
  • Fast turnaround without compromising quality
  • Direct communication and transparent processes
  • Production-ready results you can use immediately
